Private Lessons

Private dog training lessons are one hour sessions where it's just you, me and your dog.

Privates lessons are an affordable way to set you and your dog up to succeed in everyday life in your home. Be it basic obedience, task training or you have a new puppy and looking for some solid information and guidance. Each session is booked in one hour intervals at $125 an hour and can be booked to fit your schedule. We do have a 7PM cut-off for Private Lessons. That cut-off could be earlier depending on where you live.

Some of the topics we can cover in Private Lessons are:

Basic Obedience:

  • Puppy Training: Teaching sit, stay, come, down, leave it, and other basic commands to establish good communication and foundation.
  • Leash Walking: Helping dogs walk calmly and politely on a leash without pulling or lunging.
  • Recall: Ensuring dogs reliably come back when called, even in distractions.
  • Greeting Manners: Prevent jumping on people, excessive barking, or other unwanted behaviors when meeting new people or dogs.
  • Behavioral Issues:
  • Destructive Chewing: Stopping dogs from chewing on furniture, objects, or themselves.
  • Separation Anxiety: Reducing anxiety and destructive behaviors when the owner is away.
  • Fear: Addressing fearfulness towards specific triggers. We do not handle or specialize in most Aggression related cases.
  • Advanced Training and Activities:
  • Trick Training: Teaching fun and impressive tricks for amusement and bonding.
  • Sport or Activity Training: Preparing dogs for agility, obedience competitions, scent work, or other specialized activities.
  • Socialization: Exposing dogs to a variety of people, dogs, and environments to build confidence and prevent reactivity.
  • Additionally:
  • Owner Education: Learning appropriate handling, communication, and training techniques to best manage their dog.
  • Specific Problem-Solving: Addressing unique challenges like digging, barking, excessive whining, or other individual behaviors.
